  • FREE PATIENT SCRAP MAILERS

    Whether your patient has requested the return of their crown or bridge and would like to be reimbursed or your dental office generates a low volume of scrap, this program is geared specifically for recycling small quantities of dental scrap or gold jewelry.

    Four easy steps:

      1.) Order our free "starter" package of eight free prepaid bubble-lined mailers. Reorders are unlimited.

      2.) Put any amount of scrap you wish to send us in the mailer, even a single crown or bridge. There is no minimum. Gold or semiprecious dental scrap, along with gold jewelry, can be enclosed. No amalgam, please. Teeth and porcelain can be attached. Remember to close the self seal.

      3.)To ensure prompt payment, fill out the return label with the name and address where you'd like payment sent. A payment can be mailed to the d octor, staff member, or patient.

      4.) Drop the sealed mailer in a mailbox, hand it to your carrier, or bring to the post office. We pay the postage.

    To order your free patient scrap mailer package, click here. Please contact us with questions or to order more supplies. Jars are also available for larger quantities. Payment options include check or fine gold coins.

  • PAYMENT

    Payment options offered:

    • Check
    • Gold coins
    • Certified check
    • Bullion
    • Bank wire
    • Cash*

    If you choose the coin payment option, we simply convert the dollar value owed from check into your choice of coin. Shipping is always insured using either the post office's Express or Registered Mail service.

    Processing Guidelines

    Scrap payment varies based on volume and type of material received.
    Metal value determination of your scrap is pegged to the fine metal prices of the Second London Fix on the day of receipt. Our only exception, filings, sweeps andorthodontincs, which take a little longer to process and are pegged to the completion date.
