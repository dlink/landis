• MAIL IN PROGRAM

    Scrap metal mailed directly to our plant will receive our prompt attention. Upon receipt, most metals are fully processed within a week. We have afew exceptions, such as filings and orthodontics, which require a slightly longer processing schedule.

    Five Easy Steps

    1. Order our free collecting jar. It's shipped in a reusable box with a prepaid return label. We'll proviceas many jars as you need. Larger boxes are available for shipping multiple jars
    2. Put any scrap you wish to send us in a the jar.Gold and semiprecious can be mixed together. Teeth can be attached. There is no minimum. The jar doesn't have to be full. No liquids, please.
    3. Place the jar into the box.Remember to check off your payment option on the label inside the box lid.
    4. Our enclosed business reply label fits on top of the box.Fill in your name and address in the upper left hand corner. That's where we'll send your payment unless you indicate otherwise. Forgot to list your return address? Don't worry. Every office has its own account code imprinted on their label.
    5. Drop the sealed box in the mail or hand it to your mail carrier. We pay the postage.

    Processing Guidelines

    Scrap payment varies based on volume and type of material received.
    Metal value determination of your scrap is pegged to the fine metal prices of the Second London Fix on the day of receipt. Our only exception, filings, sweeps andorthodontincs, which take a little longer to process and are pegged to the completion date.
