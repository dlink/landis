ABOUT

Customer Service Available
Monday - Friday 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 pm EST

The Landis Refining Company, Inc. was originally founded as Henry Landis Inc. Since 1953, we have specialized in the purchase of scrap precious metals from the dental and jewelry industries. As a family-owned corporation, we offer two generations of expertise in the purchase and processing of precious metals.

Over the years, industry pricing has become comparable. How we differentiate ourselves is our focus on customer service. Just contact us and we will do our utmost to meet your needs: schedule pickups, ship free mailing containers, facilitate payment options, answer any questions you might have. We are here to be of assistance and offer multiple avenues of communication: phone, fax and email.

We welcome your inquiries.
